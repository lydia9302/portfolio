Hi,

My name is Borim Kim and I am a UX/UI Designer, BI Designer, motion graphic designer in Toronto, Canada. I am currently a student in Interactive Media Design of Seneca College. I used to work in WPIC as a UX Design intern in Canada and for 3 years in a digital marketing company, Digital Digm, as a UX Designer in South Korea. I planned, organized, filed, completed, and secured all documents include wireframing and proposals for making mobile applications, PC/Mobile websites. Also I managed projects as a project leader. Before getting a job in Korea, I graduated Design University. I have a double major, one is Media Design, and another is Convergence Design in Sungshin Women’s University.
