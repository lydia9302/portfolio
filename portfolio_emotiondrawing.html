Happiness, Anger, and Sadness

Emotion visualizing through hand drawing
  • SERVICE: Visual Design
  • DATE: July. 2019
  • ROLE: Hand drawing

Emotion Visualizing

Perception and Communication

Hands have a variety of functions. we can hold something, communicate (write, signal, and so on), play instruments, and so on. Not only for that, we can express or convey our emotions with hands. Human has various emotions. Even emotions are not visible things, our bodies show some emotions that are inside us. In my view, especially hands show our emotions quite a detaily. That is the reason why I love to look at other people’s hands.
I normally feel happy when I’m comfortable. When I feel cozy or comfortable, I can feel all my body is relaxed and stretched. It starts from the core of my body and through all the branches like arms and legs, finally all my fingertips are getting relaxed. Also I can feel that a field of vision of mine is brighten up a bit. The first depiction of my artwork explains not only the movement or shape of my hand, but also brightness and a field of vision.
However when I feel angry or be nervous, the muscles of my whole body starts to be hard. Normally I can feel my body starts to shrink and sometimes it trembles if I get angry too much. It seems like the whole muscle changes to bones. As I feel it, when I’m angry it’s sometimes hard to catch small things that I’ve seen. So I miss detail features of objects. The second drawing depicts the feeling that I felt.
The last drawing is about sadness. Sadness makes me to have no power on my body. All the muscles, bones, elements of my body become heavier. Even I’ve taken a shape of body, I can’t feel it.
