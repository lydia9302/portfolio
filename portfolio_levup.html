Let's level up!

Game community for newbies.

Many are turning to gaming
by the pandemic.

75%

growth in gaming traffic

20%

increase in streaming

COVID-19 has impacted people around the world by forcing them to spend more time at home, and a lot of that home time is spent playing games. Verizon has reported that since March 2020 gaming traffic has been up 75%. Streamlabs also shows a 20% increase in streaming among gamers. People are finding gaming to be a great hobby to stay connected with friends and fill time.

Newbies say,

"Which game should I play?"

New gamers are overwhelmed with choices, and there is no single resource to help them discover games that are right for them by taking in a variety of factors including skill level, genre tastes and devices owned. They are craving for trying a variety of games and want to find games that suit them, but also, they don’t have any clue which game will suit them or how to find it other than trial and error.

Gamified onboarding experience.

PERSONALIZATION

Find your thing, only for you.

Play, chat, and join the clan to level up.
