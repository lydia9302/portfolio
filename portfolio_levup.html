Lev.Up

Game Community Mobile App

INDUSTRY
Game
SERVICE
Mobile App
DATE
May. 2020
ROLE
UX · UI Design, BI Design

Background

Coronavirus has impacted people around the world by forcing them to spend more time at home, and a lot of that home time is spent playing games. Verizon has reported that since March 2020 gaming traffic has been up 75%. Streamlabs also shows a 20% increase in streaming among gamers. People are finding gaming to be a great hobby to stay connected with friends and fill time.

Persona

PROBLEM & SOLUTION

PROBLEM

New gamers are overwhelmed with choices, and there is no single resource to help them discover games that are right for them by taking in a variety of factors including skill level, genre tastes and devices owned. They are craving for trying a variety of games and want to find games that suit them, but also, they don’t have any clue which game will suit them or how to find it other than trial and error.

SOLUTION

Level Up is a game community app for adults without a broad knowledge or experience of the games. They want to try games but feel intimidated by the variety of options. This app will help the target to discover which games are suitable for them.
Level Up focuses on personalizing data to recommend suitable games for the users through fun and intuitive ways. The innovative idea for personalized recommendation is the gamifying the onboarding experience. This means, while setting your profile you are also being tested on a mini game to determine which level of the games match with your skills and personal taste. Also because users can go to buy games through this app, we expect Level Up can be another marketing opportunity for game companies.
