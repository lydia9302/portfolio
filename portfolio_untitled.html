Elimination Dance of Life

Project S. about disabled children

Anyone who has had a favorite bunny or a teddy bear which lost an ear
Those who have had a favorite blanket and still have it
Those who have imagined that the bunny may fly in the night and carry him or her on it’s back
Anyone who has seen a bright night
Anyone who has seen a dark day
Anyone who has dreamed playing with a bunny, a moon, and a whale
Anyone who loves ocean waves but cannot swim in the sea
Those who think seas are yellow
Those who think the moonlight is ticklish
Anyone who have thought that don’t want to open the eyes in the morning, even everyone is waiting for him or her
Those who are still dreaming weird, but unreal world forever
Anyone who has had people who were waiting for him or her for a long time spending their whole lives
Anyone who swallowed a clock and still lives in the past

Those who have had hard time to memorize words that people use
Anyone who has forgotten a lot of words so that has to repeat the words again and again and again
Those who have to memorize the word, ‘dangerous’ not to be in danger
Those who have to repeat the word, ‘fire’ countless times not to be in danger
Those who think ‘happy birth day’ means ‘Let’s eat some cakes with a family’
Those who have a celebration with a whole family whenever he or she memorizes one word
Anyone who always has to see crying parents
Anyone who always has to see angry siblings
Anyone who rarely sees smiling families so always wants to make them happy, nevertheless there is nothing that he or she can do
Therefore, anyone who just keep doing the thing that he or she knows
The person who has his own view
Those who are waiting for a person who is still dreaming
Those who have thought that ‘I want to be in that hard dream instead of him or her’

Anyone who is enduring hard life
But still think that life is beautiful, so it’s fine
